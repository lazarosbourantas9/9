PRESENTATION OF "Oiko Worth Living" WEBSITE

Minimal website providing information about the "Oiko Worth Living" apartment in Ioannina, Greece.

A website created to showcase the beauty of Karystos, Evia, Greece. It is designed to facilitate easy navigation and provide comprehensive information for visitors, including detailed schedules for ferry routes, current weather updates, highlights of the area and information about local events, serving as an ideal tourist guide. The site features colors inspired by nature, reflecting the untamed natural beauty of the area.
