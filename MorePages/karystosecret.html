PRESENTATION OF "KARYSTOSECRET.GR" WEBSITE

The development of karystosecret.gr prioritized a "mobile-first" philosophy, ensuring that travelers can access vital transit data and local secrets while on the move. By integrating real-time API feeds for ferry schedules and meteorological data, the platform transforms from a static gallery into a dynamic utility tool for the modern explorer.

From a design perspective, the interface employs a minimalist layout to ensure that the high-resolution imagery remains the protagonist. The palette uses deep Aegean blues and earthy olive tones—directly sampled from the Karystian landscape—to create a seamless visual transition from the screen to the physical environment of Evia.

The digital architecture serves as a curated canvas for George Lykouropoulos's vision. Every design choice was made to honor the raw textures and lighting captured in his work. This collaboration ensures that the website functions not just as a directory, but as a digital tribute to the hidden corners of Karystos that often go unnoticed by the casual traveler.

By bridging the gap between artistic photography and practical tourism information, karystosecret.gr aims to foster sustainable tourism in the region. It encourages visitors to engage with local culture and events, providing a comprehensive roadmap to the authentic Karystian experience—from its rugged mountain trails to its serene coastal escapes.
